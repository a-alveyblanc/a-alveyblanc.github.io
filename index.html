I am an undergraduate student at Northern Illinois University double majoring in Computer Science and Computational Mathematics.

My current research interests lie in the broad areas of computer security, machine learning, numerical analysis, and numerical linear algebra. Currently, I am involved in an independent study of Automatic Differentiation. This project focuses on the mathematical foundations of automatic differentiation and the performance of forward- and reverse-mode automatic differentiation in Julia and C++. The project, and all of its up-to-date details, can be found here.

My specific interests are adversarial machine learning, category theory as applied to programming languages, and sparsity detection in matrices.
